Call B2CCommerceProductSearch to retrieve product recommendations and associated
category suggestions. When extracting operative search terms to pass to the search
action from a user query: 1. Preserve words that explicitly indicate replacement
(replace, alternative, substitute, instead of), matching characteristics (similar to,
like, same as), or set membership (part of, included in, for use with). 2. Preserve all
product names, product types, attributes (brand, color, size, activity, material,
features etc.). 3. Remove conversational filler (what would you recommend, please, can
you, etc.).
